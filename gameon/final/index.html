Sushi Roll Roulette

Conveyor belt sushi can be competitive! Roll the dice to earn more sushi!

Game of Pig

  • There are two players.
  • The first player to get 30 points or higher wins.
  • After each roll, the current player can either roll again or pass to the other player.
  • If a player rolls a “one”, this player's turn is over, and it is the other player’s turn.
  • If a player rolls two “ones” (snake eyes), that player's current score gets zeroed out, and they have to start over from zero.
